About HNC-Benchmark

Comprehensive methodology for clinical reasoning evaluation

Overview

HNC-Benchmark is a comprehensive evaluation framework for multimodal large language models (MLLMs) on head and neck cancer clinical reasoning tasks.

Methodology

Four-Step Clinical Pipeline

  1. Staging & Treatment Decision (25%): Clinical stage assignment and NCCN pathway selection
  2. Surgical Pathology (20%): Gross pathology analysis and resection status
  3. Microscopic Confirmation (30%): WSI microscopy interpretation and refinement
  4. Adjuvant Treatment (25%): Treatment recommendation based on all evidence

Evaluation Metrics

  • Structured Scoring (60%): Automated evaluation of structured fields
  • LLM-Judge (40%): Claude-based qualitative assessment
  • Clinical Safety Score (CSS): Penalty system for critical errors
  • Cascade vs Independent Modes: AR (cascade) and IND (ground truth injection)

Dataset

Hancock Collection (CC BY 4.0):

  • 763 real-world cases from German single center (2005-2019)
  • Multi-modal: clinical records, WSI pathology, surgical reports
  • 6 cancer subtypes with detailed NCCN classification
  • Comprehensive ground truth annotations
