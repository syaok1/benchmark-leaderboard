Welcome to HNC-Benchmark

This is a comprehensive benchmark for evaluating the clinical reasoning capabilities of multimodal large language models (MLLMs) on head and neck cancer (HNC) diagnosis and treatment planning.

Dataset Highlights

  • 763 Cases from Hancock Collection (CC BY 4.0 open license)
  • 6 Cancer Subtypes: Oral, Oropharyngeal, Hypopharyngeal, Laryngeal (Glottic/Supraglottic)
  • Multi-modal Data: Clinical records, WSI pathology images, surgical reports
  • 4-Step Pipeline: Staging → Pathology → Microscopy → Treatment

Key Features

  • ⚕️ Clinical Safety Scoring (CSS) to detect critical errors
  • 🔬 Multi-modal reasoning with WSI microscopy patches
  • 📊 Comprehensive evaluation framework with 60% structured + 40% LLM-Judge
  • 🌐 Public benchmark with transparent scoring
